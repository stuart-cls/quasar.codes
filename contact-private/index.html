Contact

Do you find Quasar useful and would like to contribute to the project? We are always happy to form new connections.

Also, contact us if your are missing a specific component (visualization, file reader, preprocessing method, or similar) and are willing to commit resources towards its development.

Please describe your proposal or issue as thoroughly as possible. If you are proposing an addition to Quasar, please also mention a rough deadline and available resources.

The contents of this form will be sent to developers of Quasar.
